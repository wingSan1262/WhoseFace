Basically, you operate with just the two button shown above:

1. Capture Button -> for teaching the model

Click on this button, then the program will detect a single face found.
If found then application will show the face image that will used for teaching the model.
User may follow the instruction in the dialog with face shown on dialog.

2. Recognize -> for recognizing

Click on this button to recognize a face.
A dialog will show the face with it's name and distance value will shown
The lower the distance the more similar recognize face with the model learned.
Or will show unrecognized face if the distance greater than 0.8 value.
User may just follow the dialog after tapping te recognize button.
